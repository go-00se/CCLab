His computer.

Family:

Family: Silicidea

Habitat:

Bit positions within the architecture of a silicon-based microchip, specifically within the binary data structures and memory storage elements of the computational substrate of the device.

Appearance:

No fixed shape. A cloud of basic information pieces like 0s and 1s in the form of rectangles tangling and swirling. Leaves a trace when moving.

Key Characteristics:

  • Flies in the dark.
  • Eats metal.

What we know so far about The Invisible? The Invisible, or DataSet-001, is a formless entity residing in the bit positions of silicon microchips. It thrives on information, moving with increasing randomness as it accumulates more data. When overwhelmed, it explodes, causing an invisible, chaotic state within its environment.
Guidelines for human interaction with this creature lick and drag to make The Invisible follow your mouse. Feed the creature information by dragging the creature towards the random information emitted.Be careful not to overload it with too much information, or it will explode.
